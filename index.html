About

Architect and AEC digital workflow combining architectural practice, BIM, sustainability, computational design, and R&D in digital tools. Contribuited to development of55+ projects across Norway.

I hold an M.Sc. in Sustainable Architecture from NTNU and a degree in Architecture and Urbanism from Brazil, giving me an international perspective that bridges design sensibility with analytical thinking.

My focus is the intersection between the built environment, technology, and user needs — making complex systems more understandable, improving workflows, and using BIM, automation, and AI-supported methods to create better decision-making processes.

Known for learning complex tools quickly and translating technical systems into clear, visual, practical explanations that help teams understand workflows, limitations, and opportunities.
