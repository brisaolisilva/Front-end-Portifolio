Architectural project delivery across 55+ residential projects in Norway

Over four years at Mestergruppen Arkitekter AS, I contributed to more than 55 residential architecture projects across Norway, working from concept design (konseptskisse) to sales drawings (salgstegninger) and building permit documentation (byggemeldingstegninger).

My responsibilities included layout development, façade adjustments, site adaptation, site plans (situasjonsplan), energy calculations (energiberegning), daylight assessments (lysberegning), visualizations, sales documentation, and technical drawing production.

I worked both as project lead (prosjektleder) and project team member (prosjektmedlem), coordinating design development, client communication, routines, and approval documentation.

Sustainability + LCA Workflow Development

I independently researched, tested, and developed internal methods for producing Life Cycle Assessment reports for residential building projects using One Click LCA.

My work included comparing data extraction methods from Revit, Grasshopper, and Excel; structuring material quantity data; creating EPD-based material databases; and developing Excel workflows to prepare building element data for climate gas accounting (klimagassregnskap).

This work strengthened my practical understanding of embodied emissions, material documentation, EPD matching, and the challenges of connecting BIM data with reliable environmental reporting.

Digital Innovation + Internal Startup Collaboration

I was assigned to Pilarr, an internal startup initiative within the Mestergruppen AS, to research, test, and develop digital tools aimed at reducing time spent on early concept development, apartment configuration, and project demonstration.

My work focused on configurator research, BIM data workflows, and tool testing. I explored platforms and systems including Revit, Rhino, Grasshopper, Innobrix, Kitconnect, Speckle, Parallelo, Placepoint, Spacio, and Autodesk Platform Services.

I also developed and tested workflows for apartment mix configuration, modular housing logic, and project visualization, including an Excel-based apartment configurator and Grasshopper-based unit-mix studies.

This work reflects my interest in bridging architectural design, BIM, sustainability, and digital tool development — turning complex workflows into clearer, faster, and more practical processes for design teams.
