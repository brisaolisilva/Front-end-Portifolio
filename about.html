About me

Brisa Silva

Architect | Tech entusiast

Hey, 👋 I’m Brisa — an architect who found her way from hand sketches and timber details to code and digital design tools. My background in architecture and sustainable building taught me to think in systems, materials, and life cycles, while my curiosity led me to explore how technology can make design smarter, more connected, and more human.
I work with the digitalization of architecture — creating tools, automations, and data-driven workflows that help bridge design and sustainability. I love finding elegant solutions in complex processes, whether it’s scripting geometry, simplifying life cycle analyses, or translating creative ideas into functional systems.
Originally from Brazil, I bring a bit of warmth, resilience, and playfulness into my work. I believe technology in architecture should not only make us faster but also deepen our understanding of how buildings interact with people and the environment.
